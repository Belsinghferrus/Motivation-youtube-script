1 Echo chamber effect  
    Instagram oda algorithm epdi work aahuthu nu namaku therium. teriyala na naa solren. athu machine learning use pani work aahuthu. Athavathu, na oru reels upload panren, atha intha target audience kita kondu poi serkanum nu algorithm tha mudivu panum. Atha pola neenga oru video paathu, "Ah ithu nalla iruke" nu ninachi like pota. Insta algorithm ena panum na, Ivanku intha reels pudichiruku, intha reels ah ivanku anupna inum niraya paapan, instagram la ye time spent panuvaan, apdinu ninachi ungalku paatha reels related niche la videos vanthute irukum.
    Ithu tha "Echo chamber effect". 
    Nenga enaikaachum yoschu patrukingala, instagram mari niraya short videos app vanthruku example intha mojah irukatum ila sharechat. but en onum sustain panala nu. because they dint use the algorithm for personalised feed.
    Seri..enaku pudichatha instagram kaatuthu naa pakren athnala ena, nalla visayam thana nu ketinga na.
    solren, lets say oru average duration of reels 30sec. 
    unga mobile la instagram open pni settings open pnunga , atha your activity irukum atha clicking pnitu, time sent apdinu oru option irukum, atha choose panunga athla oru nalaiku evlo neram instagram la spent pnringa apdinu kaatum. lets say average ah 1 hour spent pnringa then probably around 100 reels pathrupinga. But last ah ena reels pathinga knjm nyabaha paduthi pathinga na.. onum nyabahathuku varla.
    So intha echo chamber effect ena pnuthu na.. namaku pudicha reels ah tirupa tirupa anupi..namala over consume pana vaikuthu.. all for a temporary motivation.
